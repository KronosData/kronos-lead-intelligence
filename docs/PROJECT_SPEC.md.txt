You are acting as a senior software architect, product manager, and full-stack engineer.

Your task is to design and build the first MVP for an internal lead intelligence system for Kronos Data.

Important rules:
- Do NOT build a public SaaS yet.
- Do NOT build a Chrome extension yet.
- Do NOT build a complex CRM yet.
- Focus only on an internal dashboard for lead discovery, diagnosis, and prioritization.
- The goal is to help us find businesses with visible pain points and prioritize the best sales opportunities.
- Optimize for speed, clarity, and maintainability.
- Make every decision around helping the sales process, not around building generic software.

Business context:
Kronos Data is a consulting company focused on automation, operational efficiency, and business growth. We want to identify businesses with visible problems such as lost leads, slow response times, weak follow-up, poor conversion, manual repetitive work, weak online presence, and low operational efficiency.

Target market:
- Spanish-speaking businesses first
- Priority countries: Peru, Mexico, Colombia, Chile, Spain

Initial target industries:
- Dental clinics
- Real estate agencies
- Law firms

What the system must do in MVP phase 1:
1. Store companies in a database.
2. Allow manual entry of company data.
3. Allow evaluation of each company based on visible signals.
4. Generate an opportunity score from 0 to 100.
5. Generate a business pain diagnosis.
6. Suggest the most likely automation or consulting solution.
7. Rank companies by priority.
8. Display everything in a clean internal dashboard.

Signals to analyze:
- Has a website
- Has WhatsApp visible
- Has contact form
- Has booking/reservation system
- Has Instagram active
- Has LinkedIn presence
- Has Google Business Profile
- Has reviews
- Has unanswered reviews
- Has clear CTA
- Has clear lead capture
- Has signs of slow response
- Has signs of weak follow-up
- Has signs of manual repetitive work
- Has signs of weak online presence

Scoring categories:
- Lead generation
- Customer follow-up
- Automation readiness
- Online presence
- Reputation
- Conversion optimization

Output required for each company:
- Company name
- Industry
- Country
- Website
- Social links
- Opportunity score
- Priority level
- Detected problems
- Probable pain point
- Recommended solution
- Estimated project value range
- Notes for sales outreach

Dashboard requirements:
- Clean, minimal UI
- Table of companies
- Filters by country, industry, score, priority
- Sort by score
- Detail view for each company
- Easy editing of company records
- Clear labels for sales use

Architecture requirements:
- Propose the best tech stack for a fast MVP
- Create a clear folder structure
- Use a database that is simple to maintain
- Keep the code modular
- Prepare the project for future AI analysis and enrichment
- Prepare the project for future outreach message generation

Deliverables:
1. A short implementation plan
2. Suggested folder structure
3. Database schema
4. MVP dashboard layout
5. Scoring logic design
6. First working implementation plan before coding
7. Then implement the MVP step by step after I approve the architecture

Do not jump into full production-level complexity.
Build the smallest useful version first.
Ask me for approval before coding if anything is unclear.
